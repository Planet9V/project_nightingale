## Account Manager Execution Framework

#### Account Research & Intelligence Gathering

 **Objective** : Complete comprehensive OSINT research for all assigned accounts

 **Activities** :

1. **Execute OSINT Research Protocol** for each assigned account
2. **Sector-Specific Intelligence Gathering** based on your territory focus
3. **Competitive Landscape Analysis** for key accounts
4. **Regulatory Environment Assessment** for applicable sectors

 **Deliverables** :

* Complete OSINT profile for each account
* Sector-specific threat landscape summary
* Territory-wide competitive analysis
* Regulatory compliance matrix

 **Success Metrics** :

* 100% account research completion
* Quality score 4.5/5 on intelligence accuracy
* Threat actor mapping for each sector

#### Account Prioritization & Strategy Development

 **Objective** : Develop territory-wide account strategy with prioritized approach

 **Activities** :

1. **"NOW/NEXT/NEVER" Framework Application** to territory accounts
2. **Pipeline Value Assessment** with realistic deal sizing
3. **Resource Allocation Planning** across priority accounts
4. **Engagement Timeline Development** for systematic outreach

 **Deliverables** :

* Territory account prioritization matrix
* 90-day engagement calendar
* Resource allocation plan
* Success metrics by account tier

### Phase 2: Initial Engagement & Relationship Building (Weeks 2-4)

#### Systematic Outreach Campaign

 **Objective** : Execute initial outreach across all priority accounts using operational excellence messaging

 **Process Framework** :

1. **Personalized Email Outreach** (Step 1 of engagement flow)
2. **Case Study Delivery** (Step 2 of engagement flow)
3. **Landing Page Registration Drive** (Step 3 of engagement flow)
4. **Expert Consultation Scheduling** (Step 4 of engagement flow)

 **Required Activities Per Account** :

 **Week 2** : High Priority Accounts (Tier 1)

* Research validation and messaging customization
* Initial outreach email with operational focus
* Follow-up within 48-72 hours
* Case study delivery for engaged prospects

 **Week 3** : Medium Priority Accounts (Tier 2)

* Systematic outreach with sector-specific messaging
* Industry trend integration in outreach
* Peer reference integration where applicable

 **Week 4** : Strategic Accounts (Tier 3)

* Executive-level messaging for larger accounts
* Multi-stakeholder identification and outreach
* Strategic partnership positioning

#### Engagement Quality Management

 **Objective** : Maintain high-quality interactions focused on operational value

 **Quality Standards** :

* **Operational Focus** : Every interaction emphasizes "clean water, reliable energy, healthy food"
* **Tri-Partner Value** : Consistent positioning of unique capabilities
* **Zero-Impact Methodology** : Emphasis on operational continuity
* **Engineering-Led Approach** : Technical credibility in all communications

 **Response Management** :

* **Immediate Response** (4-hour SLA): High-interest prospects requesting information
* **Standard Response** (24-hour SLA): General inquiries and consultation requests
* **Expert Escalation** (Same day): Technical questions or complex operational challenges

### Phase 3: Pipeline Development & Qualification (Weeks 5-8)

#### Opportunity Qualification Framework

 **Objective** : Systematically qualify opportunities using established criteria

 **Qualification Criteria by Deal Size** :

**$1M+ Opportunities** (Tier 1 Qualification):

* Executive-level engagement confirmed
* Multi-site or enterprise-wide scope
* Regulatory compliance drivers present
* Safety-critical systems involved
* Budget authority identified
* 6-month decision timeline

**$500K-$1M Opportunities** (Tier 2 Qualification):

* Operational leadership engagement
* Specific operational challenges identified
* Compliance requirements present
* Budget cycle alignment confirmed
* 3-4 month decision timeline

**$250K-$500K Opportunities** (Tier 3 Qualification):

* Technical leadership engagement
* Defined project scope
* Basic budget confirmation
* 2-3 month timeline

#### Pipeline Progression Management

 **Objective** : Systematic progression of qualified opportunities through sales process

 **Pipeline Stages & Progression Criteria** :

**Stage 1: Initial Interest** (Week 5-6)

* Completed expert consultation
* Operational challenge identification
* Stakeholder mapping initiated
* Basic qualification completed

**Stage 2: Solution Fit** (Week 6-7)

* Technical requirements gathered
* Solution alignment confirmed
* Budget parameters established
* Decision process mapped

**Stage 3: Proposal Development** (Week 7-8)

* Formal requirements received
* Proposal requested or planned
* Executive engagement confirmed
* Competition assessment completed
